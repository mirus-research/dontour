# DonTour

**DonTour** is an intelligent travel guide application for Rostov-on-Don and the Rostov region.

The app combines offline maps, smart navigation, and AI-powered recommendations
to help users explore cities, cultural landmarks, and natural attractions
in a comfortable and engaging way.

## Features

- **Interactive map**
  Discover cultural, historical, and natural points of interest in one place.

- **Offline access**
  Download maps and content in advance and use the app without an internet connection.

- **AI assistant**
  Answers questions, recommends places to visit, and helps plan daily activities.

- **AI-powered routes**
  Automatically generated routes based on interests:
  architecture walks, family trips, gastronomy tours, and photo routes.

- **Detailed place cards**
  Descriptions, photos, links, and precise geolocation for each location.

- **Smooth UI and animations**
  Clean design with fluid transitions and interactive elements.

- **GPS integration**
  Accurate location tracking and nearby recommendations.

- **Local smart services**
  Assistance with navigation, venues, events, and city activities.
